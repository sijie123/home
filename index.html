Experience / Notable Projects

International Olympiad in Informatics | Host Technical Committee

2018 -

I will be part of the Host Technical Committee for Singapore IOI 2020, and will be responsible for managing the translation process during IOI 2020. I have also visited Azerbaijan IOI 2019 and assisted in the Host Technical Committee for IOI 2019.

National Olympiad in Informatics | Operations/Technical Committee

2017 -

I have been part of the technical committee in organising NOI 2017-2019, and will continue to be in the technical committee for NOI 2020. The NOI is modelled after the International Olympiad in Informatics. The technical committee manages the judge and ensures integrity of the competition.

MrJudge | Developer

2017 -

MrJudge is an online judge developed with two of my close friends. Originally intended as an internal training platform, it has now expanded and used by over 1,500 trainers and students. It contains training problems from past programming contests.

National University of Singapore | Teaching Assistant

2017 -

I have gained experience as a Teaching Assistant for the following courses:
CS2040: Data Structure and Algorithms (4 iterations)
CS2030: Programming Methodology II (1 iteration)
CS1010: Programming Methodology I (1 iteration)
I was placed on the Honour List of Student Tutors for my teaching excellence.

TinkerTanker | Intern / Freelance Trainer

2016 -

I developed the GetHacking iOS app, a digital maker project in collaboration with IMDA Singapore. The one-stop app contains tools to allow anyone to program a micro:bit from scratch. I also worked on some hardware projects, including a real -life Keep Talking and Nobody Explodes clone, built with Arduino and Raspberry Pi.
After the internship, I stayed on as a freelance trainer to teach programming.
